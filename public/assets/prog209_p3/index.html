Welcome to the Stayin Alive Game

  1. Collect all diamonds on the game grid. Every diamond collected will give you ONE EXTRA second of game time.
  2. Once you have collected all diamonds, a KEY will appear randomly on the map.
  3. Grab the KEY. An EXIT will appear randomly.
  4. Head to the EXIT. But hurry, you only have 17 seconds initially to get out or you will DIE!
  5. At Every Level increase, speed INCREASES and 5 seconds is reduced from the starting time.
